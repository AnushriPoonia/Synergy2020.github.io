IGNITE-24

Starts in

IGNITE - 2024 will be an exhilarating celebration of athletic prowess of women in sports, graced by many renowned sports personalities and numerous athletes from colleges across India. With a fervent belief in the transformative power of sports, IGNITE not only encourages physical fitness but also fosters a harmonious equilibrium between academic excellence and robust health. Through a myriad of sports, games, and activities, from track and field to team sports, ‘IGNITE’ is a celebration of womanhood, sportsmanship, and the pursuit of excellence.




ABOUT US

Synergy, IGDTUW's Sports Society, is a passionate group dedicated to providing student athletes with opportunities, equipment and infrastructure. Since our establishment in 2009, we've been on a mission to transform the sports culture on our campus.
1. Synergy proudly boasts a diverse array of sporting teams, featuring talented members from our student body. We organize a variety of exciting events throughout the year to rejuvenate both students and faculty members, providing them with a break from their monotonous routines.
2. Our ultimate goal is to foster a sense of community, promote physical well-being, and uplift young women's talent in sports.
